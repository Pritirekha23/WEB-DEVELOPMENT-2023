ABOUT BTS

  • BTS, also known as Bangtan Sonyeondan, is a South Korean boy band that has taken the world by storm.
  • BTS consists of seven members: Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook.
  • The group is known for their energetic performances, catchy pop music, and socially conscious lyrics.
  • BTS has become one of the most successful K-pop groups of all time, breaking records and winning awards around the globe.
  • The group's music often tackles themes like mental health, self-love, and societal pressure, and their message of hope and positivity has inspired fans around the world.
  • BTS has broken numerous records, including becoming the first K-pop group to reach number one on the US Billboard 200 chart and the first Korean act to win a Billboard Music Award.
  • The group's fanbase, known as the "ARMY," is known for their passionate support and dedication to BTS.
